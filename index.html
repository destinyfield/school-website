At Destinyfield International Schools, we believe every child carries an extraordinary destiny. Since May 2009, we have been the trusted partner for families who demand more than ordinary schooling — an environment where academic rigour meets Godly character, and every learner is seen, known, and championed.

Our broad curriculum and modern teaching methodologies prepare students for global competition, while our Triple "F" method — Firmness, Fairness and the Fear of God — anchors everything we do.

Brief Profile of the Director

D.I.S

Pastor (Dr.) Ene Belema Secondus

Founder & Director

Pastor (Dr.) Ene Belema Secondus is a seasoned minister, counselor, educationist, dietician and a queen mother. She is a practical teacher and preacher of God's word; a devoted and exclusive trainer and motivational speaker whose teachings and counseling had tremendously blessed millions of children, youths, adults and families globally.

As an indwelling of the Holy Spirit, having great passion for children, youths and families alongside the less privileged ones, she is a philanthropist, an uncommon mentor and a role model.

She is the founder and Senior Pastor of one of the special leading Women Ministries — "The Truevine Women International Outreach". She is also the founder and coordinator of the programme "Kids without Limits".

As a minister, she was divinely mandated to establish the Destinyfield International Schools (where every child matters), which started in May 2009 and had grown over the years, producing reliable scholars with good morals, influential and updated for a better society.
