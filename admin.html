Welcome

Update your website's photos and contact details — no coding needed.

How this works

Three simple steps, every time you want to update the site.

1

Make your changes. Use the menu on the left to update photos on the Home page, About Us page, Gallery, or update phone numbers, address, email and social media links.

2

Download the update file. Click the gold "Download Update File" button at the bottom of the screen. This saves a file called content.json to your computer (usually in your Downloads folder).

3

Upload it to your website. Log in to your hosting account (cPanel / FTP), find the content.json file in your website's main folder, and replace it with the new one you just downloaded. The website updates automatically — no other files need to change.

Already have a content.json from before? Load it below first, so you're editing your current site instead of starting blank.

Load your current content file (optional)

If your website already has a content.json file, select it here to bring your existing photos and info into this tool before editing.

Good to know

A few tips to keep things running smoothly:

• Use clear, well-lit photos — square or landscape photos work best for most spots.
• You can leave any image blank if you don't want to change it.
• Always keep a backup copy of your downloaded files in case you need to go back.
• Changes only take effect after you upload the new content.json to your website.
